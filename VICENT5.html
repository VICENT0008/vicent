United Kingdom
Address
Mercedes-Benz Cars UK Ltd Delaware Drive Tongwell Milton Keynes MK15 8BA
Directions
If you’re going to drive to the Mercedes head office you need to take the M1 until you reach turn off 14 then follow onto the A509. Follow the A509 onto Tongwell Street until you reach the roundabout and take the first exit onto Dansteed Way, you will then reach another roundabout where you will need to take the 3rd exit onto Brickhill Street and then turn off onto Delaware Drive. If you’re going to take public transport to the head office you can take the 1, 30, 31 or C10 buses to a road closeby to the head office. If you would prefer to get the train you can go to either the Wolverton train station or Milton Keynes Central train station. You can take West Midlands Trains to Wolverton or the Southern, Virgin Trains or West Midlands Trains to Milton Keynes Central.
